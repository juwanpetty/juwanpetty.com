Here are some rules I use to keep my code clean and readable:

- Use [descriptive names](https://codingwithroby.substack.com/p/clean-code-the-art-of-clean-naming)
- Avoid [magic numbers](https://eslint.org/docs/latest/rules/no-magic-numbers)
- Prefer early returns
- Prefer [objects over long parameters](https://github.com/jagreehal/eslint-plugin-prefer-object-params)
- Avoid mutating variables
